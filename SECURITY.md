# Security & Privacy

## Privacy by design

Sotto processes all audio **locally on your machine**. It makes no network calls at runtime, has
no accounts, and collects no telemetry. The only network access is a **one-time, user-initiated
model download** from the official OpenVINO mirror on Hugging Face. Your microphone audio and
transcriptions never leave your PC; history is stored only in `%LOCALAPPDATA%\Sotto`.

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you find a security issue, please **do not open a public issue**. Instead, use GitHub's
[private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/smirk-dev/sotto/security/advisories/new)
or email the maintainer. You'll get a response as soon as possible.

Please include steps to reproduce and the affected version. Thank you for helping keep Sotto safe.
